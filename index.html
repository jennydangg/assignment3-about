about

Ariana Grande-Butera born on June 26, 1993 in Boca Raton, Florida is best referred to as the vocalist of our generation. She is an actress, singer, songwriter and producer. She started off her career in acting at a very young age. From the age of 13, she was on Broadway's musical "13". Later on in her career, she was on the hit show Victorious which aired in 2010. After Victorious, she starred in another Nickelodeon show called "Sam and Cat".

In 2011, Ariana Grande started her music career by signing with Republic Records. Following this, on December 12, 2011 she released her first single "Put Your Hearts Up". Her debut album, "Yours Truly" was released on August 30, 2013. This album debuted at number one on Billboard's top 200. After the success of her debut album, Ariana went on to heavily focusing on her music career. She would later release 7 other studio albums which would all be successful.

After the end of her Sweetener World Tour, she began to focus on practicing for her audition for the live movie, "Wicked". In 2021, she announced on Instagram her role in the new movie as Galinda, the Good Witch. Wicked is set to release on November 22, 2024. Ariana is currently dedicating her time to acting and she states that she is slowing down on the popstar side of herself.
